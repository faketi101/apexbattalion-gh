ABOUT US

The Apex Battalion is a community of gamers where we encourage to build up strong bond among our members regardless of their skill level, game preference or platform. We want to establish the fact that gaming can not only be entertaining but it can also be as much productive and educative as any other sports. Keeping this in mind, we have formed our very own eSports teams to promote and establish the fact that eSports has the potential to become a career choice. Our community and eSports teams will grow together with the motto "Gaming, is the way of life."
